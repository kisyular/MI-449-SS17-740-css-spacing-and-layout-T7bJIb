Welcome to Galaxy Rush

WELCOME TO GALAXY RUSH

DOWNLOAD

Galaxy Rush is an exciting endless runner where you are a bold spaceship pilot trying to be the first and cross the asteroid field. You have two handy power-ups at your disposal -- a shield and laser -- but every time you use one of your abilities, your ship goes haywire and your controls reverse! Find out how far you can go while dodging asteroids and flipping controls!

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys to move and jump
  • N / Alpha 1 to use Shield
  • M / Alpha 2 to use Laser!
  • Return to continue!
  • Escape to escape

Objective

  • Collect energy
  • Avoid asteroids
  • Beat your high score!

A chicken farmer on a remote island has lost all of his chickens! Your goal is to get them back using the seeds in your arsenal to grow plants that will help you traverse the terrain of these treacherous isles. Along the way, you may get help from one chicken that is a cut above the rest...
